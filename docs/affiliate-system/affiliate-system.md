# Partner Program

From your profile, navigate to the **Partner Program** menu to view the users you have invited.

When your payout balance reaches the threshold, select one of the invited profiles and click the **Start process** button to initiate the payout procedure.

Next, enter your billing information, and click the **Payout** button to finalize and complete the balance payment.

---

## Managing Your Invited Users

- View the list of users you have invited to the Partner Program.
- Track your accumulated payout balance.

## Initiating a Payout

1. Select an invited user whose profile you wish to process a payout for.
2. Click **Start process** to begin payout.
3. Enter your billing details as required.
4. Confirm and complete the payment by clicking **Payout**.

---

Keep your billing details accurate to ensure smooth payout processing.
