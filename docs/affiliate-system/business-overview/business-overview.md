# Business Overview

## Komashi Platform Overview

Komashi is designed to serve **small and medium-sized businesses** around **300.000USD in annual revenue**.

### Getting Started
- Businesses can **start using Komashi for free**
- There are **no upfront fees**
- Costs are only incurred **after invoices are issued**

---

## Pricing Model for Businesses

Komashi uses a performance-based pricing structure:

- Every month, the **largest 10 issued invoices are free**
- Fees are calculated based on the **net value of issued invoices**

### Fee Structure
- From **0 to 150.000 USD** in annual revenue:
  - **5.5% of the net invoice value** during the **first 3 years**
- After **3 years**, the platform fees are reduced to **approximately half**

This model ensures low entry barriers while scaling fairly with business growth.

## Important Partner Considerations

- The Partner Program is **not lifetime-based**
- To maintain a steady income, partners must ensure a **continuous flow of new referrals**
- Long-term partner success depends on **ongoing acquisition**, not single large deals

---

## Summary

- Komashi is free to start, pay-as-you-grow
- SMEs benefit from predictable, revenue-based pricing
- Partners earn meaningful commissions for a limited time
- Sustainable earnings require continuous partner activity

This structure aligns incentives between Komashi, businesses, and partners while keeping the ecosystem fair and scalable.
