# Payouts

The **Payouts** section allows you to track your commissions and request withdrawals.

---

## Payout Overview

For each payout period, you can see:

- **Month**  
  The month the commission was generated.

- **Currency**

- **Amount**

- **Status**  
  Indicates whether the payout is:
  - Pending
  - Available for payout
  - Paid

- **Actions**  
  View details or initiate a payout.

---

## Requesting a Payout

When a payout is marked as **Available**:

1. Open the payout details.
2. Click **Request Payout**.

The system will process the payout using the billing and bank details you provided.

---

## Payout Conditions

- Payouts are processed **monthly**
- A minimum payout threshold may apply
- Billing details must be completed before requesting a payout

---

## Tracking Your Earnings

Use the payouts list to:
- Monitor monthly commissions
- Track payment status
- Review historical payouts

This ensures full transparency and control over your partner earnings.

---

The Payouts section is your central place to manage and receive income from the Komashi Partner Program.
