# Browse Services

The Marketplace is your hub for discovering and connecting with service providers on the Komashi platform.

## Accessing the Marketplace

To view available providers:

1.  Navigate to [komashi.com](https://komashi.com).
2.  Click on the **Marketplace** button located in the main navigation menu.

## What You Can Do

Once you are in the Marketplace, you have access to the full network of registered providers. Here you can:

-   **Browse Providers**: View a comprehensive list of all verified service providers.
-   **Find Services**: Search for specific services that match your needs.
-   **Connect**: Initiate contact or view profile details of potential partners.

Use the Marketplace to explore the ecosystem and find the right solutions for your growth.
