# Managing Your Profile

After logging in, click the profile button located at the bottom left corner of the interface, then select **Settings**. 

In this section, you can:

- Edit your name and personal details.
- Manage how providers can contact you.
- Configure your notification preferences.
- Delete your profile if you wish.

Use these settings to keep your account information up to date and control your communications effectively.

---

## Navigating to Profile Settings

1. Log in to your account.
2. Locate and click the profile icon at the bottom left corner.
3. Choose **Settings** from the menu.

---

## Editing Your Profile Details

- Update your name or other personal information.
- Adjust preferences for provider contacts.
- Set notification options to your liking.

---

## Deleting Your Profile

If you want to permanently remove your profile:

- Go to the **Settings** page as described above.
- Find the option to delete your profile and follow the prompts.

Deleting your profile is irreversible. Make sure to back up any important information before proceeding.
