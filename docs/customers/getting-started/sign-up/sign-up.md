# Sign Up

Creating a Komashi account is quick and simple.

---

## Registration Steps

1. Visit **Komashi.com**.
2. Click the **Register** button.
3. Enter your **email address**.
4. If you have a **referral code**, you can add it at this step.
5. Submit the form.

---

## Email Verification

After registration, you will receive a confirmation email.

- Open the email
- Click the **confirmation link** to verify your account

This step is required to activate your account.

---

## Get Started

Once your account is confirmed:

1. Click the **Launcher** button.
2. Start exploring and enjoying all the benefits Komashi has to offer.

Your account is now ready to use.
