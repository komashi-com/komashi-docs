# Help & Support

This guide explains how to access help, documentation, and customer support in the Komashi platform.

---

## Accessing Help and Support

1. Log in to the **Komashi Control Center**.
2. In the top navigation bar, you will find the following options:
   - **Learn**
   - **Documentation**
   - **Support**

Choose the option that best fits your needs.

---

## Getting Support

If you encounter any issues or problems, select the **Support** menu option.

- Our support team is ready to assist you
- Responses are typically provided **within 24 hours**

---

## Help Center

In the **Help Center**, you can find articles and guides relevant to your use case.  
These resources are designed to help you solve common issues quickly.

---

## Live Chat

If you get stuck or need immediate assistance, use the **Chat** feature to contact our team directly.

Our colleagues are happy to help and guide you through any issues you may encounter.
