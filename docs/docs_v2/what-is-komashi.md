Komashi is subscription software.

# What does Komashi do?

Komashi manages:

- Subscriptions
- Payments
- Invoices
- Access (QR code)

Works for physical and digital businesses.

---

# Who can use it?

## Digital platforms
SaaS, courses, gated content.

## Gyms & studios
Memberships, visit passes, QR check-in.

## Coworking spaces
Monthly plans, credits, day passes.

## Clubs & communities
Member fees and access control.

# How it works

## Step 1 – Create plans
Set subscriptions and limits.  
Example: 10 visits per month.

## Step 2 – Connect payment
Add payment and invoice provider.

## Step 3 – Set access
Enable QR entry or digital access.  
System tracks usage automatically.
