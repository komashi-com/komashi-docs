## Who is a Partner?

A **partner** is a person or company that refers new providers to Komashi and earns a commission on the fees those providers pay.

If you know businesses or individuals who could benefit from using Komashi, you can refer them and get paid for it.

---

## How It Works

1. **Join** the Partner Program and receive your unique referral link
2. **Share** the link with potential providers in your network
3. **Earn** a commission once a referred provider signs up and starts using Komashi
4. **Repeat** — your commission grows as your referred providers grow

Your commission is a percentage of the fees Komashi charges to the providers you refer. There is no cap — the more providers you bring in, and the more they grow, the more you earn.

---

## Who Can Become a Partner?

- **Consultants and agencies** that work with businesses
- **Influencers and content creators** in the business or SaaS space
- **Communities** with access to service providers
- **Anyone** who wants to earn by recommending Komashi

---

## What Partners Do NOT Do

- Partners do not handle payments or billing
- Partners do not manage provider accounts
- Partners do not need a technical background

In short, a **partner refers, earns, and grows** — no product to sell, no service to manage.
