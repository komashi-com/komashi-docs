# Account Setup

This guide explains how to create and activate your Komashi account.

---

## Step 1 — Open the registration page

Go to [https://komashi.com/register](https://komashi.com/register).

On this page, you can start creating your account using your email address.

---

## Step 2 — Enter your email address

1. Type your email address in the form.  
2. If you have a referral code, you can enter it in the same form.  
3. Click **Continue** to submit your registration.

---

## Step 3 — Verify your email

You will receive a verification code by email.  
Enter this code on the login page to activate your account and sign in for the first time.

---

## Step 4 — First login

After verification, you will be redirected to the main dashboard.

If this is your first time logging in, you may be asked to complete a short setup process (organization details, preferences, etc.).

---

## Troubleshooting

If you do not receive the verification email:
- Check your spam or promotions folder.
- Make sure you entered your email address correctly.
- Try requesting a new verification code.

If the problem persists, contact support.
