# Quick Start Guide
# Create Your First Service

This guide walks you through the essential steps required to publish your first service and enable payments on the platform.

---

## 1. Create Your First Service

Start by creating your first service in the Admin Dashboard.  
Make sure the service is fully configured (name, description, pricing) before proceeding with payment setup.

---

## 2. Verify Your Payment Method

To activate payments, you must **confirm a payment method**.

- A **bank card verification** is required
- A temporary charge of **€1** will be made to your card
- This charge is used only to **verify your card** and may be refunded depending on your bank

Once the verification is complete, your card will be ready for use.

---

## 3. Set Up an Invoicing Provider

Choose the invoicing provider that best fits your business needs.

- Select the invoicing solution you are most comfortable with  
- Alternatively, choose the provider that best matches your legal or regional requirements

Proper invoicing setup is required for compliant billing and financial records.

---

## 4. Select a Payment Provider

Next, choose the payment provider(s) you want to use to receive payments.

- Select the payment provider you prefer
- You can **activate multiple payment providers** if needed
- This allows flexibility for different regions, currencies, or customer preferences

---

Once these steps are completed, your service will be fully operational and ready to accept payments.
