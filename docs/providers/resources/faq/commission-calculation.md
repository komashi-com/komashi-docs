---
title: Commission calculation
tags: [subscription-details, subscription, financial-settings]
question: "How exactly is the commission calculated?"
---
Go to **Komashi Subscription → View Details** and you can see the commission calculation details.
The first 5 invoices are free, then the commission is calculated as follows:
- 5.5% from 0 – 50 000 000 HUF sales revenue
- 4.5% from 50 000 001 – 100 000 000 HUF sales revenue
- 3.6% from 100 000 001 – 500 000 000 HUF sales revenue
- 3.05% from 500 000 001 – 1 000 000 000 HUF sales revenue
- 2.6% from  1 000 000 001 – HUF sales revenue

After 3 years of partnership, the commission will be reduced.
- 2.5% from 0 – 50 000 000 HUF sales revenue
- 2.25% from 50 000 001 – 100 000 000 HUF sales revenue
- 1.8% from 100 000 001 – 500 000 000 HUF sales revenue
- 1.5% from 500 000 001 – 1 000 000 000 HUF sales revenue
- 1.3% from  1 000 000 001 – HUF sales revenue
