---
title: Limit coupon usage
tags: [coupon-settings]
question: "Can I limit the number of times a coupon can be used after creation?"
---
Yes, you can limit the number of times a coupon can be used after creation. Go to **Manage Coupons** and click the **Edit** button next to the coupon you want to limit. Then click on **Limit coupon usage** button. Click on **Update Coupon** button to save your changes.
